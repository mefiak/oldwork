Video Library App for SmartTV - NinatekaTV

1.  Reason / baseline / project background

As the National Film Archive (and part of it - the National Audiovisual Institute), we want to reach a new group of recipients who have TVs and set-top-box devices. We have an ambitious repertoire, films, etudes and theater performances (6000+ films) that we would like to provide to a new audience, perhaps not having access to this type of entertainment

2.  Goals and measures

The key goal is to create an application "for TV" on one of the selected platforms: Samsung TV, Android TV, tvOS (Apple) and reach the solution to the inhabitants of smaller towns.

Indicators on the basis of which the success of the project will be determined:

10,000 movie plays on the platform per month (monthly), 3 months after launch

Average time of user watching the movie: 10.5 minutes

3.  Brand / Product / Industry

Ninateka.pl is a video-on-demand service with classic Polish animations, documentaries, performances and concerts belonging to the canon of Polish (high) culture. The website is free and its basic goal is to spread culture and education among Polish citizens and those interested in Polish culture.

4.  Customers / target group

In the context of this project: Polish citizens with smart TV devices in the 25-70 group from smaller towns.

6.  Activities to date

In the past, applications for the Samsung platform have been created

(http://szymonadamus.pl/polska-kultura-w-smart-tv-test/) and on Netia platform (https://my.netia.pl/pr/292294/ninateka-nowa-aplikacja-na-netia-playerze). Both solutions, despite meeting the basic goals of culture promotion, are technologically outdated and have been withdrawn from the platforms.

Therefore, there is a need to create a modern solution, tailored to current standards.

Ninateka also tried to develop the Orkiestrownik, an application containing musical works - unfortunately, the solution did not find recognition among users.

7.  Assumptions / expectations / mandatory elements

- The key assumption is the maximum interactivity (and speed of action) of the created prototype

- The application should have the option of playing only video files (movies, performances, documents)

- The application is to have a mapping of the film category from ninateka.pl

- The application is to allow watching free movies (payment option is not expected)

- The application is to have different categories

- Movies can be repeated within different categories

- The application information architecture should be very flat (maximum 3 levels)

- To navigate the solution should be enough remote control containing 4 arrows (4pad), OK button and Back button

- To enter the text in the solution, use the virtual keyboard displayed on the screen (the prototype should allow its use)

- The application is to have a movie search function

- The application will not use voice commands

- The application should take into account the option of interrupting the movie and its resumption after restarting the device

- The app must include views: home page, movie view, search results view, and movie status during playback - they should not be the only existing views

- The application is to have an attractive homepage that clearly presents a wealth of content

- The film view should have, as a minimum: photo, trailer, description, information about the director, actors, film category, duration, etc. and similar productions

- The movie view should allow the transition to a view (e.g. Search results) containing all the productions of the actor, director, etc.

- The application is to have a page with the history of films viewed

- The application should not contain registration


BRIEF

Funkcje:

- odtworzenie filmu

- odtworzenie zwiastunu

- wznowienie filmu

- przeszukiwanie biblioteki filmowej za pomocą okna szukaj przy użyciu klawiatury z ekranu

- szukanie filmów za pomocą kategorii

- znalezienie informacji na tematu filmu

- wyszukanie innych filmów w których grał dany aktor

- znalezienie innych filmów autora

Prototyp aplikacji na tym etapie nie zawiera możliwości logowania / przełączania pomiędzy użytkownikami. Wyświetlany kontent dotyczy i wyłącznie opcji darmowych. Szata graficzna została stworzona na podstawie strony internetowej ninateka.pl oraz wytycznych od samsung-a dotyczących aplikacji na platformę tizen. Użyta czcionka - samsung one oraz wielkości czcionek są również sugerowane przez firmę samsung. Prototyp domyślnie został zaprojektowany do użytkowania za pomocą 4 strzałek (góra, dół, prawo, lewo) oraz przycisku ok, ale ze względu problematyczności przy zaaplikowaniu tej funkcjonalności nie została ona wprowadzona.  Odtwarzanie zwiastunów w tle oraz filmów powinno następować automatycznie,  ale ta funkcjonalność nie zawsze działa (przyczyny niezależne ode mnie). Biblioteka zawiera 3 filmy oraz 2 zwiastuny.
